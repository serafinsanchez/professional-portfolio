## Overview

CleanerSpaces addresses the challenge of finding reliable, local cleaning services by creating a centralized platform where residents and businesses can discover, compare, and connect with verified cleaning professionals. The platform serves as a bridge between service providers and customers, creating a transparent, efficient marketplace for cleaning services across Colorado.

## User Flow

1. **Location Entry:** User enters ZIP code or selects city from dropdown
2. **Service Selection:** Choose from residential, commercial, or specialized cleaning options
3. **Provider Matching:** System filters available businesses based on location and service type
4. **Compare & Review:** View detailed profiles, read reviews, and compare providers
5. **Price Estimation:** Use dynamic calculator to estimate service costs
6. **Provider Selection:** Select preferred cleaning service and initiate contact
7. **Booking Process:** Connect directly with chosen provider for scheduling
