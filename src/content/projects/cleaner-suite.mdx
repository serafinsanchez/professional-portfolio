# Cleaner Suite

A modern, full-stack web application designed to help cleaning businesses manage their operations efficiently. From scheduling appointments to generating invoices, Cleaner Suite streamlines the entire business workflow.

## Project Overview

Cleaner Suite addresses the common pain points faced by small to medium cleaning businesses: manual scheduling, paper-based invoicing, and poor customer communication. The platform provides an all-in-one solution that's both powerful and easy to use.

## Challenges & Solutions

**Challenge**: Complex scheduling conflicts with recurring appointments  
**Solution**: Implemented a sophisticated algorithm that checks for conflicts across recurring patterns and provides intelligent suggestions for alternative time slots.

**Challenge**: Real-time updates across multiple devices  
**Solution**: Integrated WebSocket connections using Pusher for instant synchronization of schedule changes and notifications.
